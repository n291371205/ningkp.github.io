Biography

I got my B.E. and M.S. degrees in computer science from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2019 and 2022, respectively, advised by Prof. Sheng-Jun Huang. Moreover, I was a research intern in Tencent AI Lab and Tencent ARC Lab> for half a year respectively. My research interests include machine learning and data mining. Specifically I am working on active learning and reinforcement learning.
